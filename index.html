CRYSTAL COLLECTORS

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number shown in the green box below. You lose if the score goes above the given number. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time the game starts, the game will change the value of the crystals.
